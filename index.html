FAQs

When should I RSVP by?
+
Please RSVP by March 15th, 2026. This helps us finalize catering and seating arrangements.
What should I wear?
+
The dress code is semi-formal. Think garden party elegance with comfortable shoes for dancing!
Can I bring a plus-one?
+
Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate guests listed on your invitation. Please check your RSVP for details.
Will there be transportation provided?
+
Yes! We'll have shuttle service from select hotels to the venue. Check the Transfer & Accommodation page for details.
Are children welcome?
+
We love your little ones, but this will be an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives you a chance to relax and enjoy!
What about dietary restrictions?
+
Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when you RSVP. We'll make sure everyone is well-fed!
What time does the ceremony start?
+
The ceremony begins at 4:30 PM. We recommend arriving 15-20 minutes early to find your seat.
Is there parking available?
+
Yes, there is parking at the venue. However, we encourage using our shuttle service for convenience.
Can I take photos during the ceremony?
+
We have a professional photographer capturing every moment. Please enjoy the ceremony unplugged - we'll share all photos afterwards!
Will there be vegetarian/vegan options?
+
Absolutely! Please indicate your dietary preferences when you RSVP and we'll ensure you have delicious options.
What's the best way to get to Toledo from Madrid?
+
The high-speed train from Madrid to Toledo takes just 30 minutes. Check our Transfer & Accommodation page for detailed travel information.
